Secondhand

Secondhand is an application to organize secondhand events. Sellers can register their products they want to sell at the secondhand event. The day before the event sellers ship their products to the secondhand location and the items are marked as shipped. During the event the items are sold to customers and the items are marked as sold in secondhand. After the event lists of the sellers are printed with the revenue listed.
